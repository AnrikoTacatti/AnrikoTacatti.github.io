SEO Basic Areas

The SEO basic areas are divided into two parts.

  • On-page – the on-page search engine optimization includes the elements of the website. These could be the use of proper and more keywords, textual content, HTML code, and images.
  • Off-page – the off-page search engine optimization particularly include the concept of backlinks that is the reference of other websites through it.
The White Hat SEO

The webmasters consider the white hat SEO as the good one. It is the type that uses the methodology and tactics to improve the rankings of a particular website in a search engine that is not according to the guidelines of the famous search engines. 

The search engine optimization level of this type is on high on the scale as it is a technique that includes the development of higher quality content on the websites. It optimizes the HTML as well restructures the website to get the outreach in the context of good content. This type of optimization takes the time to create better opportunities for the website, but the end results are the most favorable ones.

 

The Black Hat SEO

This type of the SEO uses the weaknesses of the search engine algorithms to get better ranks of the websites in the search engines. This type is concluded to be not the good one as the methodology of getting higher ranking are not according to the guidelines of the search engines but are opposite of that. The level of honesty regarding this type is extremely low, as this type links spam to the websites. They stuff the keywords, use cloaking, as well as hidden text and links are used to create better rankings. The results of better rankings are quick but unpredictable. The growth of the website in the rankings might be for shorter periods.

The Gray Hat SEO

People do not discuss another type of SEO because it is particularly not a type but the indefinite use of other types. It involves most of the companies and their websites. The websites usually fall in the gray area because of the incomplete use of the basic types. It could be due to the designs or the pressure of the clients to give better results and rankings of their websites in the search engines. The techniques that have been used to make the website rank higher in the search engines do not fall into the category of white hat or black hat but somewhere in between them.

This category is affordable by the companies to get an optimal ranking of the websites in the search engines. It may take longer or shorter period. The results could be unpredictable because of the questionable techniques used for the website.
